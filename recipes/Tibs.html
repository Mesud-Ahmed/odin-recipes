Shekla Tibs

Description:

Historically, tibs was served to pay a compliment or show respect to someone.

It comes in a variety of forms, varying in type, size or shape of the cuts of meat used.

Ingredients:
  • Sliced beef or lamb
  • pan-fried in butter
  • garlic
  • onion
  • spices
Steps:
  1. Place the meat pieces in a bowl and season with salt and a portion of the berbere spice mix.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil or niter kibbeh in a large skillet or pan over medium heat
  3. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic to the hot oil
  4. Once the onions are soft, add the marinated meat to the skillet and cook
  5. Sprinkle additional berbere spice mix over the cooked meat, and serve!
