Tegabino

Description:

A lightly spiced chickpea or bean purée, shiro is particularly favored by Ethiopians on fasting days

One of the most unassuming dishes you’ll encounter, it can appear as not much more than slop. Don’t be deceived, it’s very tasty!!

Ingredients:
  • minced onions
  • oil and butter
  • garlic
  • bean purée flour
Steps:
  1. Heat the niter kibbeh (or vegetable oil) in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the finely chopped onions to the skillet and sauté until they are soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and continue to cook for another minute or until fragrant.
  4. Continue to cook the Shiro Wot over low heat, stirring occasionally, until it thickens to your desired consistency.
  5. Once the Shiro Wot is cooked to your liking,serve it with injera
